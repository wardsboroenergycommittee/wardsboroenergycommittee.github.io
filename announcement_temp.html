Get Signed Up for Rebates and Get Your Home Weatherization Questions Answered By ButtonUp Vermont

On October 26th, the same day as the Gilfeather Turnip Festival, the Wardsboro Energy Committee and ButtonUp Vermont will be at the Wardsboro Town Office to help sign people up for weatherization programs & rebates. A representitive from ButtonUp Vermont will be available to answer questions and talk one-on-one about weatherization topics.

We will also have informational flyers explaining what rebates are available and how to sign up in case you would rather do so from home.

Swing by and save money + energy!
